World Environment Day

World Environment Day 2025 celebrations with a global theme ‘Ending Plastic Pollution’ were conducted on June 5, 2025. As a part of celebrations, Poster Design and Essay Writing competitions were organized on June 9 and 10, 2025, respectively.

National Science Day

National Science Day is celebrated in India every year on 28th February to commemorate the discovery of the Raman Effect by Nobel Laureate Sir C.V. Raman in 1928. The theme for National Science Day 2025 is “Empowering Indian youth for global leadership in science and innovation for Viksit Bharat”.

World Quantum Day

April 14 is celebrated as a world Quantum Day to enhance the understanding and awareness of quantum science and Technology. As a part of this, Departments of H&S (Physics) and CSE of CVR College of Engineering has organized a six-day faculty development programme on Quantum Computing during 7-12 April 2025. There were all together 13 talks by eminent speakers from across the world.
